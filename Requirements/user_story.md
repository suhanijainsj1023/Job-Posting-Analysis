**As a** Job Seeker, Career Transitioner, or Workforce Analyst  
**I want** to explore a Power BI dashboard that consolidates fragmented job market data into a single, interactive interface, providing both high-level market insights and drill-through details for specific job roles  
**So that** I can understand compensation benchmarks, hiring trends, work conditions, and platform distribution to make informed career or recruitment decisions.

---

## ðŸŽ¯ Dashboard Objectives

- **Compensation Analysis**  
  - Show yearly and hourly salary ranges with averages.  
  - Compare median salaries across roles, regions, and platforms.  
  - Highlight outliers (extremely high/low salaries).  

- **Work Conditions**  
  - Percentage of jobs offering remote work.  
  - Percentage of jobs not requiring a degree.  
  - Percentage of jobs with health insurance benefits.  

- **Geographic Distribution**  
  - Map visualization of job density across countries/regions.  
  - Drill-through capability to explore specific regions and roles.  

- **Hiring Platforms**  
  - Breakdown of job postings by platform (LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, Jooble, BeBee).  
  - Identify which platforms dominate hiring for each role.  

- **Job Schedule Type**  
  - Full-time vs. contractor distribution.  
  - Trend analysis of contract vs. permanent roles.

## âœ… Acceptance Criteria

- Dashboard must allow **drill-through by job title** (e.g., Data Engineer, Data Analyst, Data Scientist).

- Interactive map showing **global job distribution** with filters by platform and schedule type.  
- Comparative visuals (bar charts, scatter plots, pie charts, trend lines) for platform usage and work conditions.  
- Exportable insights for HR and career planning.
